Experiencing London

When students study abroad they often spend many of their weekends getting away to explore Europe. Although, I did have weekends away I made sure I set aside many weekends to explore the city. I visited many shops, markets, clubs, and even attended a couple concerts! I had some of my best nights in London; nights that made me want to stay here foreverâ€¦
